Our Catering


Corporate

We have experience in PR and press events, cocktail receptions, grand openings, product launch, VIP nights, luncheon meetings, gala dinner buffet and more. We only use the freshest and finest ingredients and are devoted to creating the atmosphere and taste that meet the highest standards of our clients.

Private

We can cater for housewarming parties, birthdays, anniversaries, private dinners, BBQ etc. Hosting a party has never been easier. Our menus are very flexible so as to meet individual taste, budget and theme. You can simply sit back and enjoy your special occasions with family and friends.

Wedding

We offer a full range of service to meet the requirements of each bride and groom, including lunch/dinner buffet, cocktail receptions, wedding cakes and favours, table decorations, floral arrangements, hired equipment and glassware, bar services and more so you can just relax and celebrate your big day.

Children

Our unique children's menu will help busy parents to host a fun, impressive and memorable party for their children. All our food are homemade and freshly prepared to meet any dietary requirements. We can offer themed birthday cakes, decorations, tableware and party bags plus chocolate fountain, candy floss, popcorn and hot dog machines.
