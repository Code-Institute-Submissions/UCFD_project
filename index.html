Our Story


2Rooms' Cafe

Our cafe in Happy Valley was opened from 1999 till 2012 and became a popular hangout in the neighbourhood offering a wide range of home cooking and cakes, specialty coffee and signature drinks, including refreshing mint tea, homemade lemonade, our own mixed fresh juices to quench your thirst and "real & thick" milkshake like banana cookies, to satisfy your sweet tooth.

2Rooms' Kitchen

We have moved onto catering service with the setup of a fully licensed, well-equipped professional kitchen from 2004 till now. Aiming to provide clients with a high quality service plus creativity and variety, our catering service covers all scales of branded events, media functions, private dinners, poolside BBQ, children's birthday as well as wedding parties.
